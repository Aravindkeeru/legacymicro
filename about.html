Who We Are

R&A Supply Solutions Pvt Ltd, operating as Legacy Micro, was established in May 2026 in Nehru Nagar, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India. We set out with a clear mission: to become India's most trusted independent distributor for semiconductors and electronic components.

As an independent distributor, we specialize in sourcing hard-to-find, obsolete, and end-of-life (EOL) electronic components from a vast global network of trusted suppliers spanning China, the USA, and the European Union.

In a world where semiconductor supply chains can leave procurement teams waiting 24–48 hours for a simple availability check, we decided to change the game. Legacy Micro is built to deliver instant answers and rapid fulfillment — eliminating uncertainty from your sourcing process.

  • India's first independent distributor with instant part search
  • Global sourcing from China, USA, Europe & Asia Pacific
  • Specializing in hard-to-find, obsolete & EOL components
  • Rigorous quality assurance and anti-counterfeit protocols

Our Mission

Our mission is to make electronic component sourcing transparent, fast, and stress-free for engineers, procurement managers, and manufacturing teams across the globe.

We believe that no production line should halt because of a missing component. Whether it's a current-production IC, a hard-to-find passive, or an obsolete connector that went end-of-life years ago — Legacy Micro has the network and expertise to find it.

By combining deep industry relationships with cutting-edge search technology, we empower our clients to source with confidence and speed — turning what used to take days into minutes.

  • Dedicated support for hard-to-find & obsolete parts
