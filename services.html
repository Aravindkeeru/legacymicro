Comprehensive Component Solutions

Global Component Sourcing

Leverage our worldwide supplier network to source any electronic component — from the latest semiconductor releases to legacy passives. We maintain direct relationships with authorized distributors, OEM excess inventory holders, and vetted independent suppliers across every major region.

Whether you need 10 pieces or 10 million, our sourcing team works around the clock to find the best combination of price, lead time, and quality for your specific requirements.

Hard-to-Find Parts

Allocation constraints, extended lead times, and global shortages don't have to halt your production. Our specialized sourcing team excels at locating components that others can't — even during the tightest market conditions.

We use proprietary data, deep industry relationships, and real-time market intelligence to identify available inventory worldwide. When standard channels fail, Legacy Micro delivers.

Obsolete & EOL Components

Product lifecycles in electronics are getting shorter, but your legacy systems still need support. Legacy Micro specializes in sourcing discontinued, end-of-life (EOL), and not-recommended-for-new-designs (NRND) components that manufacturers no longer produce.

Our global network of excess inventory sources, last-time-buy specialists, and aftermarket suppliers ensures you can keep legacy systems running without costly redesigns.

Quality Assurance

Every component sourced through Legacy Micro undergoes rigorous inspection and testing. Our multi-stage quality assurance process is designed to detect counterfeits, verify authenticity, and ensure components meet original manufacturer specifications.

We follow industry best practices aligned with AS6081, AS6171, and IDEA-STD-1010 standards. Full documentation and traceability come standard with every order.

Visual Inspection Functional Testing Anti-Counterfeit Traceability

BOM Analysis & Support

Upload your Bill of Materials and let our experts analyze it for risk, cost optimization, and alternative sourcing opportunities. We identify potential supply chain risks before they become production-halting problems.

Our BOM analysis service includes lifecycle status checks, cross-reference suggestions for obsolete parts, multi-source qualification, and competitive pricing benchmarks — giving you a complete picture of your component supply health.

BOM Review Cost Optimization Alternative Suggestions Risk Assessment

Excess Inventory Management Solution

Turn your idle or obsolete components into working capital. We leverage our extensive global network and major B2B platforms to actively market and promote your excess inventory to inbound buyers worldwide — entirely at our expense.

From carrying out all necessary testing and certification to managing customer communication, we handle the full sales and shipping process. Focus on your core business while we maximize the return on your surplus stock.
