Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Effective Date: July 2026  |  Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Legacy Micro (operated by Legacy Micro), we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company name provided through our forms.
  • Inquiry Details: Part numbers, quantities, and component requirements submitted via search, quote requests, or BOM uploads.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and search queries for analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your component sourcing requests and RFQs.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you quotations, pricing, and availability updates for requested parts.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • Suppliers: We share part requirements (not personal data) with our global supplier network to fulfill your sourcing requests.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (e.g., Formspree for forms, Cloudflare for hosting) that may process data on our behalf.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including HTTPS encryption, secure hosting on Cloudflare, and limited access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Opt out of any marketing communications.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., supplier pages, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
